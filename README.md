# Amazon Sales Analytics Suite

## Executive Overview

The **Amazon Sales Analytics Suite** is a multi-page Power BI reporting solution designed to deliver a comprehensive, data-driven view of Amazon’s global sales operations.

Built on a clean data model with optimized **DAX measures**, **Power Query transformations**, and interactive visual design, this dashboard suite converts raw transactional data into actionable insights for sales leadership, category managers, and operations teams.

The solution is organized into three core analytical views, each answering a distinct business question while maintaining consistency in KPI logic, layout, and navigation.

---

## Dashboards Included

### 1. Global Sales Overview

**Purpose**  
Provide a high-level snapshot of worldwide sales performance, profitability, and product mix.

**Key Features**
- Top-level KPIs: Units Sold, Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Cost  
- Derived metrics: Unit Price and Unit Cost  
- Revenue and profit by item type (e.g., Cosmetics, Office Supplies, Baby Food)  
- Regional performance across North America, Europe, Asia, Sub-Saharan Africa, and other markets  
- Sales channel split: Online vs Offline contribution to revenue, profit, and units sold  

**Business Value**  
Enables leadership to rapidly assess global performance, identify high-value categories, and understand where profitability is concentrated geographically and operationally.

---

### 2. Performance & Trend Analysis

**Purpose**  
Identify long-term demand patterns, pricing behavior, and operational trends.

**Key Features**
- Units sold over time (2011–2017) highlighting seasonality and growth trends  
- Average metrics: revenue, cost, profit, unit price, and units sold  
- Order priority distribution (Critical, High, Medium, Low)  
- Revenue contribution by item type  

**Business Value**  
Supports forecasting, inventory planning, and operational decision-making by uncovering demand cycles and priority-driven sales behavior.

---

### 3. Category, Region & Channel Insights

**Purpose**  
Deliver deep-dive analysis into product margins, regional pricing, and channel efficiency.

**Key Features**
- Unit price comparison across global regions  
- Total cost and total profit by item type  
- Profitability comparison by sales channel (Online vs Offline)  
- Profit by order priority and item type  

**Business Value**  
Helps category managers optimize product strategy, regional teams tailor pricing, and operations teams improve fulfillment efficiency.

## Data Model & Technical Architecture

### Data Sources
- Amazon sales transactional dataset  
- Fields include:
  - Item Type
  - Region
  - Order Date
  - Units Sold
  - Unit Price
  - Unit Cost
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Profit
  - Order Priority
  - Sales Channel

### Modeling Approach
- Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query  
- Calculated columns and DAX measures for all KPIs  
- Logical grouping of measures for clarity and maintainability  

### DAX Highlights
- Profitability calculations  
- Channel-based segmentation  
- Time-series aggregations  
- Category-level KPI measures
